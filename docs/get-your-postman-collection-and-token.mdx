title: Get Your Postman Collection and Token
---

You can easily and quickly get your Postman collection through the portal with your user-specific token already set up in your end-points through the Datafiniti Portal!

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Settings Page">
    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-a829270c-4408-45aa-90cb-a8cad2967955/doc-82f0e261-4f6b-45af-9f00-b0b805bf07d2/1775452423520-dgtvgbzcnpd-5881759-image.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1920" height="1080" alt="Screenshot showing accessing the Settings page" noCaption />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Your Postman Collection File">
    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-a829270c-4408-45aa-90cb-a8cad2967955/doc-82f0e261-4f6b-45af-9f00-b0b805bf07d2/1775452425925-6n260w2avo5-d593636-image.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1920" height="1080" alt="Screenshot showing 2. download your postman collection file" noCaption />

    <Callout kind="tip">
      **Your Own Custom Postman Collection**

    When you download your Postman Collection through the portal, **your specific user token is already integrated into your collection!**
    </Callout>

    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-a829270c-4408-45aa-90cb-a8cad2967955/doc-82f0e261-4f6b-45af-9f00-b0b805bf07d2/1775452427603-m7rdtg6nkol-aaa173f-image.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1920" height="1080" alt="Screenshot showing 2. download your postman collection file" noCaption />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import Your Postman Collection Into Postman">
    <Callout kind="info">
      **Need to download Postman?**

    You can download Postman [here](https://www.getpostman.com/).
    </Callout>
