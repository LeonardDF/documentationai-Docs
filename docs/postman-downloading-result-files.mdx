---
title: 'Postman: Download Result Files'
---
<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Download ID" title-type="p">
    After running your query with `"download": true`, you can grab your `download ID` from the response you get in Postman.

    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-a829270c-4408-45aa-90cb-a8cad2967955/doc-82f0e261-4f6b-45af-9f00-b0b805bf07d2/1775452488706-5utt779f5ze-a7bc70c-8.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1920" height="1080" alt="Postman response showing download ID after running a query" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the Get Download Endpoint" title-type="p">
    Click "Get Download" in the "Downloads" folder to begin the download process.

    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-a829270c-4408-45aa-90cb-a8cad2967955/doc-82f0e261-4f6b-45af-9f00-b0b805bf07d2/1775452490900-ksnk9e33xun-ec23cab-2.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1920" height="1080" alt="Get Download endpoint in the Downloads folder" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Request Your Result File(s)" title-type="p">
    Replace `:id` with your `download ID` number you got from Step 1. In this example, the `download ID` was `40`.

    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-a829270c-4408-45aa-90cb-a8cad2967955/doc-82f0e261-4f6b-45af-9f00-b0b805bf07d2/1775452493062-nbay9c8vfvb-bbd5f87-3.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1920" height="1080" alt="Replacing :id with download ID number in the request" />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download the Files" title-type="p">
    Copy the URL listed in the `"results"`.

    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-a829270c-4408-45aa-90cb-a8cad2967955/doc-82f0e261-4f6b-45af-9f00-b0b805bf07d2/1775452494821-fww69qkmjva-323305c-4.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1920" height="1080" alt="Response showing results URL to copy" />

    <Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
      **Result Files**

      Result files are generated for every 100mb of records. Depending on the number of records downloading and your view, you may have many result file links, not just one.
    </Callout>

    Paste the results URL into your browser.

    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-a829270c-4408-45aa-90cb-a8cad2967955/doc-82f0e261-4f6b-45af-9f00-b0b805bf07d2/1775452499231-uw7qrnsm5e8-a092287-5.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1920" height="1080" alt="Pasting results URL into browser address bar" />

    Hit Enter to trigger the download. Then enjoy your dataset!

    <Image src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-a829270c-4408-45aa-90cb-a8cad2967955/doc-82f0e261-4f6b-45af-9f00-b0b805bf07d2/1775452503264-8fe5zqck6t6-a6da573-6.png?fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat" width="1920" height="1080" alt="Download triggered in browser" />
  </Step>
</Steps>
